My Dedication to Self-Study

My dedication to learning extends beyond formal academic settings into an extensive self-study journey, driven by a desire to deepen my understanding and prepare for advanced research. This continuous, independent pursuit has equipped me with a robust foundation in mathematical concepts and methods, which complements my academic and research goals.

Topics Studied Independently

Over the past few years, I have proactively explored advanced topics that align with my research interests and aspirations:

  • Algebraic Topology: I studied concepts like homology and cohomology using resources such as "Algebraic Topology" by Allen Hatcher. This exploration enhanced my grasp of topological spaces and their properties.
  • Topological Data Analysis (TDA): Merging my interest in topology and data science, I studied persistent homology and applied these principles to complex data sets through research articles and lecture notes by experts like Pr. Vidit Nanda.
  • Abstract Algebra: I expanded my knowledge beyond basic definitions to group theory, ring theory, and field theory, using "Abstract Algebra" by Dummit and Foote to develop a deeper understanding.
  • Quantum Information and Computing: To bridge mathematics with modern applications, I explored topics in quantum computing with "Quantum Computation and Quantum Information" by Nielsen and Chuang, focusing on quantum gates, algorithms, and information theory.
  • Graph Theory: Preparing for future research on Graph Neural Networks, I studied graph properties and algorithms relevant to machine learning applications.

Impact of Self-Study on My Academic Path

My self-study efforts have:

  • Enhanced Research Capabilities: Prepared me for complex projects such as my Hopfield Networks survey and Ising model research.
  • Broadened Interdisciplinary Insights: Reinforced my belief in mathematics as a versatile, unifying tool across various scientific domains.
  • Enriched Teaching Skills: Supported my roles as a teaching assistant and tutor, enabling me to explain advanced concepts effectively and guide students through challenging material.

Self-study has been an essential part of my journey, complementing formal education and paving the way for future academic and research endeavors. This commitment ensures I am well-prepared for rigorous master's programs and positions me for success in my ultimate pursuit of a PhD and a career in academia.
