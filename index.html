Welcome

Welcome to my personal website! I am Jorys Mahamba, an aspiring academic with a deep-rooted passion for mathematics, theoretical physics, and computer science. My academic journey has been shaped by a commitment to interdisciplinarity, showcased by my pursuit of dual degrees, interdisciplinary research projects, and proactive self-study.

I believe that mathematics is not just a field but a bridge connecting diverse scientific disciplines. This perspective has driven me to engage with topics that span pure mathematical theory and its applications, from gauge theory and topological data analysis (TDA) to quantum information and machine learning.

Ongoing Project

Currently, I am authoring a survey article on Hopfield Networks. This work dives into the mathematical structures and applications of these networks in artificial intelligence, reflecting my commitment to blending theoretical research with practical advancements in technology. This project is in review for potential publication in "La Gazette de la Société Mathématique de France".

Research Work

Explore my contributions, including research on the Ising model, which demonstrated phase transitions and provided insights into statistical mechanics, as well as my internship at BRED Banque Populaire, where I collaborated on refining the mathematical foundations of machine learning models for financial applications.
